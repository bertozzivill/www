About

Amelia is a disease modeler and data visualizer based out of Seattle, Washington. She currently works as a Post-Bachelor Fellow at the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation, and collaborates with the HIV and Ebola teams at the Institute for Disease Modeling.
